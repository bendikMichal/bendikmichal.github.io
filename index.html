Rectangle w/ circle collision detection

There are two most well known collision detection algorithms: AABB collision detection (between two rectangles) and circle collision detection.

However, the main topic on hand is the collsion detection between circle and rectangle.

How can one know (programmatically) whether in the image below the rectangle and the circle have collided?

Probably the first and the easiest solution would be to construct a square around the circle. While this approach would "mostly" work, it would come at the cost of accuracy in the corners.

The most correct approach would be to also construct a circle around the rectangle. Now, if the red and white circle collide AND at the same time the white rectangle collides with the red one, we can say that the white rectangle collides with the white circle
